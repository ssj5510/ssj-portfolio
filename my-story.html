My Story

Hello from Colorado! I’m Steph, a designer and solopreneur based in Fort Collins.

I spent nearly a decade working for a Fortune 100 company doing graphic and web design, communications, and marketing, mixed with some freelancing to hone my skills and experience the world of startups. I now support companies of all sizes while venturing on my solopreneurship journey.

I believe brand strategy is the heartbeat of an organization, and pumping a high quality flow of information is the blood that keeps it alive. I curate beautiful and effective designs rooted in marketing and communication strategy to help companies drive audience engagement, increase awareness, align overarching goals and leave a lasting impact—all within the user's average attention span of 2 minutes or less!

Outside of design, you can find me reading, singing, hanging out with my family, and taking photos (mostly of my dogs and the Rocky Mountains).
